Stress

Your reaction to stress, formally considered anxiety,
can either have a detrimental or manageable
effect on you personal health and wellness.This is
why it's important to recognize it, identify what's
causing it, and find good ways to handle it.

SOME FACTS AND FIGURES:
- 25% of teens (age 13-18) experience anxiety disorders
- Suicide is the 2nd leading cause of death in adolescents
- Almost 1 in 5 Americans struggle with mental health problems
each year (18.5% in U.S.)
