Mental Health

Mental health largely impacts how
we think, process our feelings, and
react to everything around us.

Being that mental health is so
important, there are several warning
signs that you may be able to identify
in yourself or those around you. If any
of these habits sound familiar, you or
someone else may have poor mental health.

WARNING SIGNS:

< Eating/sleeping too much/little, lacking energy (more so than normal), feeling isolated, withdrawing from typical activities, experiencing unexplained pains/aches, having extreme mood changes, yelling/fighting with family and friends, trouble calming down or getting thoughts out of your head, etc.
