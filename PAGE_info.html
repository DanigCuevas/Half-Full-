Information

Mental health is incredibly important in having
a balanced lifestyle & positive mindset. But, it
all starts with you understanding what mental
health is and recognizing your personal habits!

Mental health pertains to an individual's social,
emotional, and psychological well-being.

Stress is your body's way of responding to any
sort of perceived demand or threat (no
matter how big or small it may be).

There are many methods to improve and
maintain positive mental health and combat stress.
However, everyone has different preferences and
experiences, so that's why it's good to experiment
with what works for you best!
